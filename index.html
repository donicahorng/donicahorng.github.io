Perks Of Our Membership

Join our extremely exclusive club of ski lovers to receive a very affordable, safe, and comfortable vacation.

Access To Our Cabins

Access the top ski cabins in New Zealand at a fair price.
Chalets can be rented with the ease of a single phone call, a booking, and check-in the same day!

Member Exclusive Ski Tracks

All year long, you can use a ski track that is only available to members. The tracks and memberships are handled professionally. Enjoy the convenience of skiing to and from your chalet, which is situated next to it!

Emergency Service

24/7 emergency support team is here to help you in many different situations, as well as safety house and guards working in places near the chalets and the ski track!

Facilites

We pride ourselves on offering our guests an experience they will truly enjoy.

Sky TV

With your own Sky decoder, relax in the privacy of your own chalet while watching more than 50 channels. Enjoy your nights at your leisure while you unwind and watch your favourite show or movie.

Spa Pools

After a long day on the slopes, relax in a hot spa! between the hours of 8am and 9.30pm for use by visitors. Our spa pool facilities are open to kids as long as an adult is actively supervising them.

Sauna

We welcome visitors to our sauna, which provides space for up to 3 people to sit comfortably. Since children should not use this facility, we do enforce an adults-only policy.
