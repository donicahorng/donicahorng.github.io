KAKAPO

Up to 10 Adults / 30 Children

Our largest chalets, which are tucked away within our exclusive club, have plenty of room to relax in comfort and elegance. When you enter, the big open-concept design that smoothly connects the sizable kitchen, dining, and living areas will captivate you. Additionally, our chalets provide a wide range of first-rate amenities to make sure your stay is nothing less than exceptional. Every whim is catered to, from calming saunas to reviving spa amenities.

PUKEKO

Up to 6 Adults / 15 Children

With plenty of room and privacy for everyone, our chalets are especially made to meet your needs. Each person in your group will have their own cosy haven thanks to the spacious room layouts and several bedrooms in our chalets. We have put restrictions on the number of adults and kids per chalet since we recognise the value of a tranquil and restful vacation. Everyone will experience comfort and enjoyment as a result of this. Our chalets provide the ideal location for making priceless memories with loved ones, whether you're organising a family reunion, a meeting of friends, or a special celebration. So, assemble your loved ones and set out on an unforgettable getaway in one of our roomy, family-friendly chalets.

$599 / Night

KERERU

Up to 2 Adults / 4 Children

Our family-friendly chalets offer the best choice for a smooth and enjoyable stay. These chalets are ideal for families with 2-4 kids because they have two roomy bedrooms, ensuring that each child has their own cosy spot to unwind. Our chalets provide bedrooms in addition to a separate living room, enabling families to spend valuable time together in their own private places. There is plenty of space in the living area for games, laughter, and special occasions. So assemble your loved ones, check into one of our family-friendly chalets, and start planning an excursion that will leave everyone with lifelong memories.

$199 / Night
